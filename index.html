About Ana

Hi, my name is Ana Senra-Silva, and I am proud to be running for the Mineola Board of Education.

I am a mother of two wonderful boys—one attending Hampton Street School and another at Mineola Middle School—and like many families here, I am deeply invested in the success of our schools and our community.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Biological Sciences from SUNY Old Westbury and a Master of Science in Education from Hofstra University. As an educator in Nassau County, I understand firsthand the importance of supporting our teachers and ensuring every student has the opportunity to succeed.

I am proud of my Portuguese heritage, and I am equally proud to call Mineola home.

I am running on a platform of transparency, accountability, and a strong community voice because our community deserves open communication and meaningful input in the decisions that impact our children’s future.
